# AI Prompts

## Personalized Audit Summary Prompt

Generate a concise 100-word summary explaining:
- the user's estimated AI overspending,
- potential monthly and annual savings,
- recommended optimizations,
- and suggested next steps.

Tone:
Professional, concise, startup-oriented.

## Why This Prompt

The prompt was designed to:
- keep outputs short,
- maintain clarity,
- avoid hallucinated pricing information.

## Failed Prompt Attempts

Earlier prompts generated:
- exaggerated savings claims,
- overly verbose summaries,
- unrealistic enterprise recommendations.

The final prompt improved factual consistency.
