# User Interviews

## Interview 1

**Name:** R.D.  
**Role:** Indie Hacker  
**Company Stage:** Solo founder

### Quotes
- “I honestly forgot how many AI subscriptions I’m paying for.”
- “Most tools overlap more than I expected.”
- “I’d definitely use a free audit tool.”

### Surprising Insight
The founder valued convenience more than small cost savings.

### Design Changes
Simplified recommendation explanations.

## Interview 2

**Name:** S.K.  
**Role:** Startup CTO  
**Company Stage:** Seed-stage SaaS

### Quotes
- “We have multiple ChatGPT Team seats nobody actively uses.”
- “AI costs grew faster than expected.”
- “Benchmarking against similar startups would be useful.”

### Surprising Insight
Unused seats were a larger issue than pricing itself.

### Design Changes
Added emphasis on seat optimization.

## Interview 3

**Name:** A.M.  
**Role:** Engineering Lead  
**Company Stage:** Series A startup

### Quotes
- “We use different AI tools because different engineers prefer different workflows.”
- “Consolidation is harder than people think.”
- “A finance-friendly report would help internally.”

### Surprising Insight
Team preference fragmentation complicates optimization.

### Design Changes
Recommendations became more nuanced instead of suggesting full consolidation.
