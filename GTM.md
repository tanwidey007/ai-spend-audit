# GTM Strategy

## Target User

The ideal target user is:
- a technical founder,
- engineering manager,
- or CTO
at a seed-stage or Series A startup with 5–30 employees.

These teams often adopt multiple AI subscriptions rapidly without centralized spend oversight.

---

## Trigger Moments

Potential users would search for this tool after:
- receiving unexpectedly high AI invoices,
- scaling engineering teams,
- purchasing multiple AI subscriptions,
- or discussing cost optimization internally.

Relevant searches include:
- “reduce ChatGPT team costs”
- “best AI coding tool pricing”
- “AI subscription audit”
- “Cursor vs Copilot pricing”
