# Economics

## Lead Value

A converted customer could realistically generate between $1,000–$10,000 annually depending on infrastructure credit purchases and subscription volume.

For estimation:
- average contract value assumed: $3,000/year
- estimated gross margin: 25%

This creates approximately $750 gross profit per successful conversion.

## Path To $1M ARR

To reach $1M ARR:
- 333 customers paying $3,000 annually
OR
- larger enterprise accounts with higher contract values.

This requires:
- strong distribution,
- repeatable lead generation,
- and trust in optimization recommendations.

---

## Critical Assumptions

The business only works if:
- customer savings are real,
- recommendations are trustworthy,
- and lead acquisition remains low-cost through organic channels.
