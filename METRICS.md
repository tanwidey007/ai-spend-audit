# Metrics

## North Star Metric

Completed AI audits per week.

This metric directly measures user engagement and lead generation potential.

---

## Input Metrics

### 1. Landing Page Conversion Rate
Percentage of visitors who begin an audit.

### 2. Audit Completion Rate
Percentage of users who finish the audit process.

### 3. Lead Capture Rate
Percentage of completed audits resulting in email submission.

---

## Initial Instrumentation

The first analytics tracked would include:
- page visits,
- CTA clicks,
- audit completions,
- and lead submissions.

---

## Pivot Threshold

If fewer than 5% of visitors complete audits after significant traffic testing, the onboarding flow or value proposition would require redesign.
